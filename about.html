Self-Introduction

Bookworm

In my free time, I love to read a variety of both fiction and non-fiction books, as it offers a wide range of perspectives for me to contemplate and think. Reading allows me to dive into worlds and live many lives. George R.R Martin once said: "A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies." and I wholeheartedly agree.

Leader

I was honored to become the chairperson of my school's Library Club, and cherished every moment that I had together with my CCA mates. It was something that brought solace to me. Knowing that what I did could bring the same joy that I had when reading to others, was enough for me.

Tech-savvy

Whenever I'm bored at home, I will naturally gravitate towards tinkering with my electronic devices. After being inspired by learning Scratch in Primary School, I continued to learn basic Python coding during my free time. This year, I've just learnt about iOS app development using Swift, and have also been learning some basic courses in that.


So, what would I want to achieve with my Information Technology diploma? Besides knowing more about coding, I want to be able to make people's lives more convenient through coding. Life now is all interconnected through the internet; banking is done online, movies can be streamed online, and even dating can be done online. I hope that by joining this course, I'm able to make a positive impact on the world by developing useful tools for people to use.
