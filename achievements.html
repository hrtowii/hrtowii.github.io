Swift

  • I've always been intrigued by apps, and how they're coded for so many devices while maintaining user friendliness.
  • Recently, since the Covid-19 Pandemic started, more and more apps have been released to aid people fare through tough times during lockdown by providing various goods/services such as GrabFood, FoodPanda, and logistics companies such as Redmart and Amazon Singapore.
  • Hence, I started to search up on what languages mobile apps are coded in, and came across Swift. Swift is the native language used in Apple iPhone applications.
